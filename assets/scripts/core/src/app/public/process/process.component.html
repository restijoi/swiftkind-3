Though our projects are uniquely tailored for each client, our methodology stays consistent.

PROJECT ARCHITECTURE

In this stage, we break down the functionalities/features into small pieces of tasks and create sprints/milestones and then assign it to our developers. This helps us to plan ahead, anticipate possible blockers, and make sure on meeting the deadline. We follow the agile approach of development.

SPRINT PLANNING

This stage is divided into two sub-stages, The first one is called "Introspective" and happens before the development is started and "Retrospective" which happens on the last day of the development. We set small goals for every sprint (this compose of a group of tasks) and the goal is to finish it by the end of the sprint. We usually do one sprint per week. Plan on the first day of the sprint and evaluate the development by the end of the week.

DEVELOPMENT

At this stage, the actual coding happens. Developer(s) assigned to the project works on the small tasks that is assigned to them. The developers works on one task at a time then they push it for review and testing. The process is a cycle because each tasks are being evaluated and reviewed before moving to the next task. This will ensure that a specific feature is fully working with no issues before moving/working to a new feature.

SHIP AND MAINTAIN

When we reach this stage, it means that the development is done and the project is deployed. We honestly don't want to leave our clients hanging and so we offer maintenance contract after the project is shipped.
